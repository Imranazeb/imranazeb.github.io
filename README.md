A personal blog delivering AI-generated insights on **technology trends** and **stock market news**. Built with Jekyll and the Chirpy theme, this blog combines cutting-edge AI content generation with a clean, modern reading experience.

## 🌐 Live Site

Visit the blog at: **[aurangzeb.app](https://aurangzeb.app)**

## 📖 About

This blog features:

- **AI-Powered Content**: Articles generated using advanced AI pipelines to deliver timely, relevant tech and financial market insights
- **Focus Areas**: Technology trends, entrepreneurship, and stock market analysis
- **User-Centric**: Content tailored for readers seeking concise, actionable information
- **Continuous Improvement**: Actively incorporating reader feedback to enhance content quality
