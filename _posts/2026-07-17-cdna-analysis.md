## Analysis
CareDx, Inc. is a leading precision medicine company focused on the transplant care journey, offering transplant patients and clinicians a suite of surveillance, diagnostics, and informatics solutions. Headquartered in Brisbane, California, the company's core products include AlloSure, a cell-free DNA test designed to detect organ rejection in kidney and heart transplant patients, and AlloMap, a gene expression test for heart transplant monitoring. CareDx has carved out a specialized niche in the Diagnostics & Research industry, serving a patient population that requires ongoing, long-term monitoring — creating a recurring revenue model that investors find attractive.

The Healthcare sector, and specifically the Diagnostics & Research industry, has been in the spotlight as advances in genomics, liquid biopsy, and precision medicine continue to reshape how clinicians monitor and treat patients.
