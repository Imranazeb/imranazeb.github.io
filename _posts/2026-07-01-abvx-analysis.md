## Analysis
Abivax SA is a French clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on developing treatments for chronic inflammatory diseases. Their lead candidate, obefazimod, targets conditions like ulcerative colitis — a significant unmet medical need affecting millions worldwide. The company trades on U.S. markets under the ticker ABVX and has been building momentum as it advances through late-stage clinical trials. You can learn more about their pipeline and mission directly at https://www.abivax.com.
